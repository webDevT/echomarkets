What are spreads in trading?

Have you noticed that your trading platform always displays two sets of prices on the product you are trading?

The spread is the difference between the bid and ask prices, meaning the lower your spreads, the better. This is where Echo Markets offers traders an edge. What is a pip?

Forex spreads are measured in pips.

For most major currency pairs, you’ll find one pip equal to 0.0001. Using EUR/USD as an example, a 0.1 pip spread for the pair would be 1.1084/1.1085.
