What is a forex
Islamic account?

Forex trading is Halal and that's why we're excited to introduce our Echo Islamic account, designed specifically for traders who are looking for a halal way to trade financial markets. At Echo Markets, we understand the importance of adhering to your religious principles as a trader. Our Echo Islamic account: A swap-free account option, exclusively for our clients of the Muslim faith. Unlike traditional accounts, our Echo Islamic account incurs no swaps or interest charges on overnight positions. Instead, an administration fee is charged, seamlessly deducted from your account balance.

Just because you’re on an Islamic account, doesn’t mean Echo Markets will compromise your trading experience. Our Islamic Account offers the same tight spreads and lightning-fast execution that our regular MT4 and MT5 accounts benefit from. You'll still have access to all of the advantages of trading with Echo Markets, such as access to deep liquidity pools and cutting-edge trading tools.
